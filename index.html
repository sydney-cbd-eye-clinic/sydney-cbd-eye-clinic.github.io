About us

When it comes to diseases affecting the eye, the earlier a condition is detected, the easier it is to manage. The Sydney CBD Eye Clinic offers quality medical expertise and state of the art diagnostic tools which provide you with comprehensive assessment and management of your eye health. Our eye doctors specialise in a vast range of ophthalmic conditions including cataracts, glaucoma, diabetic eye disease, macular degeneration and eyelid conditions.

Conditions treated

Primary vision care

Regular eye exams can be considered part of your overall health care. It also helps detect any underlying conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure and more. Learn More

Cataract

Almost everyone will develop cataracts (cloudy lens) in the eye with time. We offer cataract surgery that is quick, comfortable with minimal downtime Learn More

Glaucoma

Patients with glaucoma may not be aware they have the condition and may have been quietly losing vision without them knowing. Early detection is key to preventing and managing the disease. Learn More

Diabetic eye care

Diabetes can adversely affect the health of the eye. It is important to ensure regular eye examinations and maintain vision. Learn More

degeneration

Macula is responsible for helping you see clearing in the centre of your vision. Conditions that affect this requires regular monitoring and treatment Learn More

Laser Refractive surgery

Tired of wearing glasses or contact lenses? Make an appointment to have your eyes assessed and see if laser eye surgery is for you Learn More

Eyelid malposition & cosmetic eyelid surgeries

The eyelids and the surrounding area can be prone to changes either with disease or with age alone. Learn More

Why Choose us?
Sydney CBD Eye Clinic difference

Our practice is led by Dr Dennis Lowe, who is uniquely dual trained as an Ophthalmic surgeon as well as a medical specialist in Neurology. As many eye conditions are linked to diseases related to the brain and body, it is important to approach eye care with an holistic approach.

Our team of Ophthalmologists understands this and will assess your eye condition in detail taking into account your overall health conditions. We also offer and perform the latest surgical techniques through The Sight Foundation at the Sydney Eye Hospital – one of the most prestigious centres of excellence for Ophthalmic care. Procedures we offer including minimally invasive glaucoma surgery, laser refractive correction, eyelid malposition surgeries and cataract surgeries with the vast selection of high-quality intraocular lens that suits your visual requirements.
