Payment information

The hospital fees and the anaesthetic fee are separate invoices from the surgeons fees. You may received these invoices separately. Should you have any questions regarding the fee for your procedure, please feel free to contact us directly.

Private Health insurance

We work with various private health insurances across Australia. In general, private health insurance may cover a portion of the surgeon/anaesthetic and hospital fees if a procedure is performed (depending of your level of cover and the type of procedure).
