Routine eye exams are an important way to maintain healthy vision. Our doctors are trained to detect any abnormalities that may be present and offer specialised treatments.

General eye exams

Annual eye exams are extremely useful in discovering and preventing disease, preserving healthy vision, and maintaining overall wellbeing. In fact, routine vision exams may lead to the discovery of high blood pressure, multiple sclerosis, diabetes, strokes , kidney problems, brain tumors, and conditions that affect other parts of the body. Many diseases of the eye itself do not present symptoms until they have already damaged your vision. Once your sight is lost, it is often impossible to restore. Keeping yo ur prescription updated and your eye care visits regular also helps prevent visual strain, headaches, blurred vision, and eye sensitivity. Healthy vision is priceless, and worth protecting for a lifetime. We are honored to partner with you in maintaining your best possible vision.
